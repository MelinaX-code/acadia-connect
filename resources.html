Student Resources

This page highlights useful academic, social, and daily-life resources to help students feel supported and connected at Acadia University.

Getting Started & Daily Life

Bi-Weekly Grocery Shuttle

A free shuttle service that helps students travel to nearby grocery stores such as Walmart, Superstore, and Sobeys in New Minas.

Why this helps: Ideal for students without access to a car and those new to the area.

Academic Help & Study Support

MASH (Math & Statistics Help)

Drop-in support for students who need help understanding math and statistics concepts.

Why this helps: Provides a welcoming space to ask questions and build confidence in math-related courses.

CACHE (Computing Help Centre)

A support space for programming-related courses such as Python and Java, where students can get help and collaborate with peers.

Why this helps: Supports learning while encouraging peer interaction and collaboration.
