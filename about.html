About Acadia Connect

Our Mission

Acadia Connect is an online platform that brings together international and local students to get to know each other by hanging out at different places on campus and off campus based on their profile, interests, and hobbies.

Making Friends Easier

The app is the best way to make friends at Acadia without feeling anxious about going to events or forcing yourself to socialize. It provides a natural, comfortable way to meet people from other countries and learn about different cultures.

Resources & Exploration

Beyond connecting with peers, Acadia Connect offers valuable resources for navigating administrative formalities or discovering what Wolfville has to offer. Whether you need practical information or want to explore your new community, we've got you covered.

Feeling at Home

Acadia Connect aims to strengthen the student experience and help you feel at home when you arrive at Acadia, whether you're a local or international student. We believe that every student deserves to feel welcomed, supported, and connected to their community.
